Maggi Goreng Recipe

Description

Homemade maggi goreng alternative with slighly healthier ingredients than typically prepared in mamam restaurants.

Ingredients

  • 1 Maggi noodles in packet (curry flavour)
  • 3 fried tofu
  • 1 cup Green leafy vegetables such as bok choy
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/2 packet Maggi curry seasoning
  • 1/2 tbsp Dark soy sauce
  • 1 shallot, cubed
  • 1 Lime (optional)
  • 1 sunny side up (optional)

Steps

  1. In a pot, bring water to boil and add in a packet of dehydrated Maggi noodle.
  2. Cook noodles until it soften (30 seconds). Drain the water and set aside.
  3. In a wok, heat oil and fry onions until it just begin to brown.
  4. Add tofu and vegetables and stir-fry until vegetables soften.
  5. Add an egg.
  6. Stir in cooked noodles, Maggi seasoning and dark soya sauce.
  7. Mix well with a wok spatula and stir continuously for a few minutes.
  8. Serve hot.
